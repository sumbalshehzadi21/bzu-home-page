SUB-CAMPUSES

Bahauddin Zakariya University (BZU) has several sub-campuses which offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines, including arts, sciences, social sciences, and professional fields.
Layyah Campus
Southern Punjab
Layyah Campus of Bahauddin Zakariya University in Pakistan aims to provide quality education and academic facilities to students in the Layyah region.
Vehari Campus
Southern Punjab
Vehari Campus serves as an educational outpost, contributing to the academic growth of students in the Vehari region, with a commitment to quality education and research.
Sahiwal Campus
Central Punjab
Sahiwal Campus extends BZU's academic reach, fostering education and research excellence in the Sahiwal region.The campus is dedicated to providing accessible higher education
Lodhran Campus
Lodhran Punjab
This Campus will serve the Lodhran District area and out of District Area in future to produce skilled and professional people
D.G Khan Campus
Southwestern Punjab
BZU Sub-Campus, D.G Khan upgraded as Ghazi University from Fall 2014
